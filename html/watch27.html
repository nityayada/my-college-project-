Ultima Watch Circle

RS.4000

The Ultima Watch Circle 2.0 is a feature-rich smartwatch boasting a 1.43-inch TRU AMOLED display with 2.5D curved glass, delivering vibrant visuals and an intuitive touch experience. Notably, it integrates ChatGPT 3.5, allowing users to interact with AI directly from their wrist. The watch supports Bluetooth calling, offers over 100 sports modes, and provides comprehensive health monitoring, including 24/7 heart rate and SpO₂ tracking. Its sleek design features a premium zinc alloy frame, and it is water-resistant up to 3 ATM, making it suitable for various activities. With a 250mAh battery, it delivers up to 4-5 days of normal usage and up to 30 days on standby.
