Citizen
CALIBER 0210 NC10001-58A

RS. 30000

Superior precision meets the highest standards of quality in The CITIZEN. Representing the never-ending pursuit of the essence of the watch, this new addition serves as the ideal all-purpose timepiece, perfectly balancing mechanical refinement with aesthetic intrigue. On the wrist, the watch measures comfortably at 40mm, with its silver-tone stainless steel case faceted with sharp surfaces of brushed and polished finishing and complete with an integrated bracelet. An anti-reflective sapphire crystal protects its white dial, with an electroformed process used to evoke a billowing expanse of white clouds, in turn creating a delightfully subtle interplay between light, texture, and color. Towards the top of the display is the eagle mark, symbolizing the twin ideals of The CITIZEN – foresight and dedication to the pursuit of ideals, as well as the enduring connection between watch and wearer. Inside, the mechanical Citizen Caliber 0210 is at work.
