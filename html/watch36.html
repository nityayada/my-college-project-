Casio
CA-53WPC-1B

RS. 95000

Presenting a whole collection of special collaborations to celebrate 50 years of Casio watches and get you ready for 45th PAC-MAN anniversary in 2025. With playful designs and a nostalgic touch, this lineup brings to life the world of PAC-MAN, the ever-popular game from Namco Bandai Entertainment. The watch face is designed to evoke a game stage with the familiar characters running through the maze on the game screen, and is outlined by a yellow bezel to project the look of an arcade game machine. The case back is engraved with the characters and PAC-MAN logo. The band is made with bio-based resin, a material expected to help reduce environmental impact. Comes in special packaging with an arcade game machine design. PAC-MAN™& ©Bandai Namco Entertainment Inc.
