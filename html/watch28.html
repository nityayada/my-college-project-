Utima Magic Pro

RS. 7000

The Ultima Watch Magic Pro is a feature-rich smartwatch designed for both style and functionality. It boasts a 1.96-inch TFT LCD display with a 320×386 pixel resolution and 500 nits of peak brightness, ensuring clear visuals even in bright conditions. The watch is equipped with Bluetooth calling capabilities, allowing users to make and receive calls directly from the watch. It also offers over 110 sports modes, heart rate monitoring, SpO₂ level tracking, and sleep tracking, providing comprehensive health and fitness insights. The device is built with a zinc alloy frame and features an IP67 rating for dust and water resistance, making it suitable for various environments.
