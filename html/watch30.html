Kick Aerowatch Sigma

RS.1900

The Kick Aerowatch Sigma is a smartwatch featuring a 2.01-inch Fluid HD display, delivering a bright and smooth viewing experience. It offers Bluetooth 5.4 connectivity for seamless pairing with devices, AI voice assistance for hands-free control, and multiple sports modes to track various activities. With an IP67 rating, the watch is resistant to dust and water, making it suitable for everyday use. Additionally, it provides health monitoring features such as heart rate tracking, SpO₂ monitoring, and step tracking. The Kick Aerowatch Sigma is available in multiple colors, including Cool Grey.
