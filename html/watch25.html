CMF Pro 2

RS. 29000

The CMF Watch Pro 2 is an affordable smartwatch featuring a 1.32-inch AMOLED display with auto-brightness, interchangeable bezels, and over 100 customizable watch faces. It offers Bluetooth calling with AI noise reduction, built-in multi-system GPS, and health monitoring tools like heart rate, sleep, stress, and SpO₂ tracking. With a battery life of up to 11 days, it's designed for both style and functionality.
