Citizen
Tony Stark '63 AW2075-05W

RS. 600000

Sophistication fuses with technology in the latest Marvel’s Tony Stark timepiece from Citizen. Featuring the iconic shapes of the Arc Reactor on its dial, the action-ready watch reflects the Super Hero's ingenuity and the technological edge of Stark Industries on the wrist. With up to 100 meters of water resistance, this hardwearing timepiece is as robust in functionality as its design indicates. A geometric, black IP case and a matching black leather strap with contrast stitching secure the timepiece, while on the dial, gold-tone and fiery red details further channel Tony Stark's signature aesthetic and suit of Iron Man armor. Like the iconic power source in Iron Man's mechanized suit of armor, Citizen’s Eco-Drive technology drives the watch, ensuring its continuous and sustainable operation by making use of any light source to eliminate the need for a battery. © 2023 MARVEL
