Apple Watch Ultra

RS. 59000

The Apple Watch Ultra is a rugged and feature-packed smartwatch designed for athletes, adventurers, and outdoor enthusiasts. Crafted with a 49mm aerospace-grade titanium case and a flat sapphire crystal display, it offers exceptional durability and scratch resistance. The bright Retina display (up to 3000 nits) ensures visibility in any environment, while the customizable Action Button allows quick access to workouts, waypoints, and more. Equipped with dual-frequency GPS (L1 + L5) for precise location tracking and water resistance up to 100m, it's ideal for extreme sports, including diving.
